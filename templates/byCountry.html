Suicides by Country

The Russian Federation has the highest number of suicides (1,209,742 - total number of suicides). According to research, Russia ranks high in global suicide rankings due to political and social upheavals such as the inability to reform and overcome the Soviet era. While, the United Kingdom has the lowest number of suicides (136,805 - total number of suicides). The United Kingdom is home to Samaritans, a charity that focuses on suicide prevention and anyone can reach out to this charity, free of charge, anytime. The United Kingdom goverment decided to spend 1.5 million euros to develop planning and strategies on preventing suicide.
